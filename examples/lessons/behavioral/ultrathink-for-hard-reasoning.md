**Rule:** Add `ultrathink` or `think harder` to prompts for nontrivial design, debugging, or tradeoff-reasoning tasks.

**Why:** Hard problems answered without extended thinking often turn into a propose → push back → re-propose loop. One thorough reasoning pass up front is cheaper than several rounds of correction.

**How to apply:** When asking the agent to design something nontrivial, debug a tricky issue, or reason about architectural tradeoffs → add "ultrathink" or "think harder" to the prompt.
